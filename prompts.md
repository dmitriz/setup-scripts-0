# Smart Prompting Helpers

Effective phrases to force minimal, clear, and high-quality technical solutions.

---

## 1. Minimalism Forcing

> "First list solutions by minimalism level — no libraries, minimal libraries, full libraries — and let’s choose after."

- Forces alternatives.
- Stops auto-defaulting to bloated ecosystems.

---

## 2. Functional vs Object-Oriented Choices

> "Please show the functional (no classes) version first, before suggesting object-oriented styles."

- Forces clean, composable designs.

---

## 3. Avoid Assumptions Based on Popularity

> "Please do not assume popular solutions are best — list pros/cons objectively before recommending."

- Protects against blindly suggesting trendy tools.

---

## 4. Clear Architecture Options

> "Before starting implementation, list high-level architecture options — no coding yet."

- Ensures strategic overview before writing code.

---

## 5. Trap Awareness

> "Please highlight potential hidden traps, inefficiencies, and complexity costs in the suggested solution."

- Forces assistant to think ahead and warn you.

---

# Strategy

- Force *listing* first, *picking* second.
- Favor *explicit options* over *assumed defaults*.
- Bias toward *minimal*, *functional*, *extensible* designs.
